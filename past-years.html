Past Years

 SEEK 2017

SEEK 2017 featured meticulous laser wielding robots designed to navigate through an obstacle course in a race to defeat alien invaders. Team Applesauce emerged victorious as this year's winners after a closely contested finals against team Gap. Thank you to everyone involved for making this a memorable event.

 SEEK 2016

For SEEK 2016, we ramped up the difficulty by introducing three sub-tasks to stop the Martian invasion. Our winners, "Wow I Thought", beat out the runners up, Waterloo Something, our own invaders from UWaterloo. Thank you to all of the sponsors, participants and volunteers!

 SEEK 2015

The winner of the inaugural SEEK was Cheesecake Squad! They defeated fifteen other robots in head-to-head matches, collecting the most blocks of U-243. The runners up were GM and FAD respectively. Congratulations to the winners, and thank you to all the participants.
